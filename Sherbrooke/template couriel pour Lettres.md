Template in English (for international / non-Francophone referees)

Subject: Reference letter request – AI Management Professor position (Université de Sherbrooke, posting 07823)

Dear [First name],

I hope you’re doing well. I’m writing to ask whether you would be willing to write a reference letter for my application to a tenure-track professor position in AI management at the Université de Sherbrooke (École de gestion, Department of Information Systems and Quantitative Management – SIMQG).

The position is focused on management of AI in organizations (governance and risk management of AI, AI lifecycle management / AIOps–MLOps, performance and resilience of AI systems, etc.), which aligns very closely with my recent work on AI governance, public-sector AI, and the engineering and operation of AI systems in practice.

The application deadline is 12 December 2025, and the university requires three recommendation letters to be sent directly by the referees. Practically, the letter should be emailed to:

Prof. Abdelouahab Mekki Berrada, Dean
École de gestion – Université de Sherbrooke
Job posting no 07823
Email: doyen.eg@USherbrooke.ca

If you are able to support my application, it would help a lot if you could send your letter by [propose a date: e.g. Monday 8 December], to give them time to process everything before the deadline.

In your letter, it would be particularly helpful if you could comment on:

my research contributions and potential in areas such as AI governance, AI in organizations, data/AI-driven decision-making, and AI lifecycle management;

my ability to work across research and practice, including collaborations with organizations;

and, if you have seen it firsthand, my teaching and supervision abilities.

I’m attaching my CV, research statement and teaching statement so you have the full picture. I can also send you the full job ad if useful.

Please let me know if you’re comfortable writing a strong letter in this context. Either way, thank you very much for your time and support.

Best regards,
Carlos Denner dos Santos
[email]
[phone]

You can tweak the “comment on” bullets depending on the person:

Supervisor → emphasize research trajectory & supervision.

Industry collaborator → emphasize applied work, impact, partnerships.

Co-author in governance/IS → emphasize fit with gestion de l’IA and methods.
